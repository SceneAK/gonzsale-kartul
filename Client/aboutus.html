About GonzSale

Our Story

Welcome to GonzSale! A creative project born out of a passion for storytelling, community, and the arts. Meet our team of visionaries who made this possible: Evan Kue, Ignatius Pio, and Dionisius Abiyasa.

How It All Began

Once upon a time in the bustling halls of Gonzaga High School, three talented students, Evan Kue, Ignatius Pio, and Dionisius Abiyasa, found themselves brought together by their passion for creative writing. They had noticed that their peers were full of stories, poems, essays, and articles brimming with unique perspectives—but without a proper platform to showcase them. That’s when the idea for GonzSale was born, a website dedicated to hosting and celebrating the literary works of their school.

Evan, Ignatius, and Dionisius each contributed their unique talents—Evan as the tech wizard, Ignatius as the editorial mastermind, and Dionisius as the marketing genius. Through challenges, late nights, and teamwork, GonzSale became a reality, a digital library celebrating creativity.

Quick Facts

  • Launch Year: 2024
  • Team Members: Evan Kue, Ignatius Pio, Dionisius Abiyasa
  • Mission: To nurture creativity, celebrate diverse voices, and foster a literary community.
